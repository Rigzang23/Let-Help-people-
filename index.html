LHP (Let Help People) — Description

LHP (Let Help People) is a community memecoin created to make small, practical contributions to people who need assistance — not through promises or investment talk, but through a simple mechanism: a portion of transaction reward fees is designated to be distributed to people and causes that need help. How it works: • Transactions involving LHP include a small reward fee. A part of those fees accumulates transparently on-chain. • Accumulated rewards are distributed to verified people, community projects, and assistance programs. • Community discussions help guide distribution toward genuine needs. • No promises — only real help when possible. Important: LHP is not investment advice and carries the normal risk of memecoins. The focus is on helping others through reward fees and community effort — nothing more.

Values

  • Transparency — public distributions when possible.
  • Community-first — open decision-making.
  • Practical help — small but real actions.
  • No promises — just effort to help.

How reward fees help

  • Fee collection: Small fee on each transaction.
  • Verification: Community checks and confirms needs.
  • Distribution: Funds go to individuals or causes.
  • Reporting: Transparent community updates shared.
No hype. No promises.

LHP is not a charity or investment vehicle. It’s a community effort to direct small token rewards into acts of help.

Transparency

All on-chain transactions related to LHP can be verified publicly on the blockchain explorer. The community encourages open audits and proposals for new helping mechanisms.
